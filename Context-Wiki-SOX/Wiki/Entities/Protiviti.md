Protiviti is a global consulting firm known for its long-running
SOX Compliance Survey series tracking the cost and effort of
[[SOX Compliance Program]]s.

Key facts:
- Its 2024 survey (with [[AuditBoard]]; 1,500+ audit and finance
  professionals polled) found SOX scope, hours, and costs still
  rising: 52% saw internal SOX costs increase over two years; 71%
  saw scope expand.
- 78% are not currently using AI in their SOX programs even though
  57% see high or moderate transformative potential — see
  [[AI in SOX Compliance]].
- 44% cite IT access controls and security as the area with the
  most deficiencies, often from [[Segregation of Duties]] conflicts
  and gaps in user access reviews — weaknesses in
  [[IT General Controls]].
- Presents a four-stage technology maturity model: manual;
  purpose-built [[GRC Platform]]; data analytics and automated
  controls; AI and automation.
