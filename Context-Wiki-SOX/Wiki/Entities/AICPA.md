The American Institute of Certified Public Accountants (AICPA) is
the U.S. professional body for CPAs. Its Auditing Standards Board
issues Statements on Auditing Standards, codified as the AU-C
sections of [[GAAS]], which govern audits of non-issuers (private
companies and other entities outside [[PCAOB]] jurisdiction).

Key facts:
- The AICPA Clarity Project restructured GAAS into AU-C sections
  aligned with International Standards on Auditing.
- [[AU-C 940]] (from SAS No. 130) is the AICPA counterpart to the
  PCAOB's [[AS 2201]] for an [[Integrated Audit]] of [[ICFR]] when
  a non-issuer auditor is engaged to opine on ICFR.
- The AICPA is one of the five sponsoring organizations of [[COSO]].
