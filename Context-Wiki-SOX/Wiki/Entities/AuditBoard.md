AuditBoard is a provider of purpose-built audit, risk, and controls
management software — a [[GRC Platform]] used to run
[[SOX Compliance Program]] work such as control documentation,
testing, dashboarding, and reporting.

Key facts:
- Co-hosted, with [[Protiviti]], the June 2024 webinar poll of
  1,500+ audit and finance professionals behind the 2024 SOX
  Compliance Survey on analytics, automation, and AI in SOX.
- Featured in Protiviti's four-stage technology maturity model for
  SOX compliance, where adopting a purpose-built controls solution
  (stage 2) makes layering on analytics, AI, and automation
  (stages 3-4) significantly easier and more effective.
