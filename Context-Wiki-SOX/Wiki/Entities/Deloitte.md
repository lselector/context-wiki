Deloitte is one of the Big Four audit and advisory firms. Its SOX
advisory publications argue that many [[SOX Compliance Program]]s
have not kept pace with technology and should be modernized.

Key facts:
- "SOX Modernization" (2022) identifies three transformation
  levers: operating model optimization, program enhancements, and
  technology/automation (e.g., testing up to 100% of populations
  instead of sampling, and implementing a [[GRC Platform]]) — the
  core of [[SOX Modernization]].
- "Purpose-built: SOX program operating models" (2024) maps SOX
  activities to the [[IIA]] [[Three Lines Model]] and offers three
  structural options, from SOX owned by [[Internal Audit]] (third
  line) to a dedicated second-line SOX function under the CFO/CAO.
- Promotes "Internal Audit 4.0" (assure, advise, anticipate,
  accelerate) when SOX moves out of the third line.
