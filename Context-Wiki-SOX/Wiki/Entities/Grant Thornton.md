Grant Thornton is a large international accounting and advisory
firm. In this wiki's sources it appears as the author, under
[[COSO]] commission, of COSO's Guidance on Monitoring Internal
Control Systems.

Key facts:
- Authored the January 2009 monitoring guidance (three volumes:
  Guidance, Application, Examples) with project leader Trent
  Gazzaway, under COSO chair Larry Rittenberg.
- Also led the June 2008 exposure draft of Volume III — Application
  Techniques, produced with a task force including Big Four
  partners plus GAO and [[SEC]] observers.
- The guidance elaborates [[Monitoring of Internal Control]],
  [[Key Controls]], [[Persuasive Information]],
  [[Ongoing Monitoring]], and [[Separate Evaluations]], helping
  companies "build in" rather than "add on" monitoring to cut
  [[SOX Section 404]] cost.
