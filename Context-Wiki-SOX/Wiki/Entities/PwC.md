PwC (PricewaterhouseCoopers) is one of the Big Four audit and
advisory firms and the author, under [[COSO]] commission, of both
the 2013 [[COSO Internal Control Framework]] and the 2017
[[COSO ERM Framework]].

Key facts:
- Its Middle East [[ICFR]] point of view (2021) defines ICFR, maps
  international regulators ([[SEC]], [[PCAOB]], EU, UK FRC) and
  regional mandates, and presents a three-level
  [[ICFR Maturity Model]] (regulatory compliance, process
  efficiencies, value enhancement).
- Introduced the seven-phase "FOCUSED" ICFR methodology (Framework,
  Operations assessment, Control design review, Upgrading, Sampling,
  Effectiveness testing, Documentation).
- PwC 2020 survey: 76% see [[Internal Control]] as valuable, but
  ~60% lack a dedicated [[GRC Platform]]; 2019 survey: 41% have no
  ICFR scoping process.
