The Institute of Internal Auditors (IIA) is the global professional
body for the [[Internal Audit]] profession and a [[COSO]]
sponsoring organization.

Key facts:
- Published the [[GAIT Methodology]] practice guides, including
  "GAIT for IT General Control Deficiency Assessment" (March 2008),
  a principles-based, 10-step method for judging whether
  [[IT General Controls]] deficiencies found in [[SOX Section 404]]
  work rise to a [[Significant Deficiency]] or
  [[Material Weakness]].
- Issued the [[Three Lines Model]] (2020 update), the governance
  model firms like [[Deloitte]] use to map SOX roles across
  management (first line), the SOX function (second line), and
  internal audit (third line).
