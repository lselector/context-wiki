A GRC (governance, risk, and compliance) platform is purpose-built software for managing a [[SOX Compliance Program]]: scoping, risk-control matrices, testing workflow, certifications, dashboards, and deficiency tracking.

Key facts:

- In [[Deloitte]]'s [[Three Lines Model]] mapping, the second-line SOX function implements and maintains the GRC platform; implementing a GRC tool is one of Deloitte's [[SOX Modernization]] technology levers.
- [[Protiviti]]/[[AuditBoard]]'s technology maturity model puts a "purpose-built controls solution" (GRC/audit management software) at stage 2 of 4 — enabling automated dashboards and reporting — and notes that once adopted, layering on analytics, AI, and automation becomes significantly easier.
- [[PwC]]'s 2020 survey found almost **60%** of respondents lack a specific GRC system for managing internal control.
- Vendor examples in the summaries: Oracle Fusion Cloud Risk Management and Compliance, SAP Access Control (with SAP Process Control and Risk Management), and NetSuite's native GRC features plus SuiteApp ecosystem.
