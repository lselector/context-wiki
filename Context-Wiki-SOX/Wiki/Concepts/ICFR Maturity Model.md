An ICFR maturity model rates how advanced a company's [[ICFR]] / [[SOX Compliance Program]] is, from basic compliance toward efficiency and value. Several firms publish versions:

- **[[KPMG]] SOX Health Check:** a proprietary maturity continuum measuring almost **150 qualitative and quantitative data points** across **seven pillars** — Strategy, Risk Assessment, [[Entity-Level Controls]], Control Selection, Testing Strategy, Evaluating Results, and Governance — each with lower-maturity (e.g., "basic compliance driven") and higher-maturity (e.g., "value-driven culture") characteristics.
- **[[PwC]] ICFR Maturity Landscape:** three levels assessed across four dimensions (role clarity, governance culture, data analytics, value): Level 1 Regulatory Compliance (defensive culture, sample testing), Level 2 Process Efficiencies / Controls Insights (control optimization, full-population analytics), Level 3 Value Enhancement ([[Continuous Controls Monitoring]] of critical controls, collaborative culture).
- **[[Protiviti]]/[[AuditBoard]] technology maturity model:** four stages of SOX technology enablement — Manual (MS Office), purpose-built [[GRC Platform]], data analytics and automated controls, and AI and automation.
- Common outcomes sought: lower [[Total Cost of Control]], improved governance, stronger [[Top-Down Risk Assessment]], and moving beyond a compliance-only posture.
